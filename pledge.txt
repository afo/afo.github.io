### PLEDGE (2019) ###


In agreement with the 3.5 manifesto I, Alexander Fred-Ojala ( https://keybase.io/afo ), pledge to:


1. Donate 3.5% of my income after taxes to environmental organizations striving for political change (e.g. awareness, mobilization, regulations, global carbon tax)

2. Dedicate 3.5% of my waking hours (approx 4hrs / week) to environmental and humanitarian non-profit work.

3. Not purchase new clothing articles and electronic devices.

3.5 Spread the word of the 3.5 manifesto and incentivize friends, family, and others to commit to a PLEDGE.

4. Only eat a vegan diet.

5. Limit my use of disposable plastic articles.

6. Switch to an electricity company that produces 100% renewable energy.

8. Not take any personal flights and climate compensate for all business flights.

9. Educate myself on topics of sustainability, activism, political processes, the science of climate change, carbon-reduction technology etc.

10. Become an active climate activist (by joining Extinction Rebellion)


PLEDGE COMMITTED JULY 4TH 2019.



The 3.5 manifesto is inspired by the civil resistance research conducted by the political scientist Erica Chenoweth.
